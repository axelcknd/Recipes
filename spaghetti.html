Spaghetti

Making spaghetti is really easy with these simple steps.

  1. Buy spaghetti pasta.
  2. Boil Water.
  3. Put spaghetti into the water, with a pinch of salt and oil.
  4. Cut up vegetales
  5. Cut up garlic and onion and put them in a pan
  6. Add other vegetables in the pan
  7. When pasta is soft, drain out the water and add it to the pan with vegetables
  8. Add Tomato Sauce
  9. Cook everything for another 2 to 5 minutes
